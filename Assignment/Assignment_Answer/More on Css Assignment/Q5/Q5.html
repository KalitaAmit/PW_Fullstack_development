Google font

Google Fonts is a free and widely used online library offering a vast selection of open-source fonts for web and print use. Developed by Google, it enables designers and developers to access and integrate high-quality typography into their projects with ease, without worrying about licensing restrictions. With hundreds of font families available, Google Fonts offers styles ranging from classic serifs to modern sans-serifs, as well as display fonts, handwriting, and monospace options. The platform simplifies the font selection and embedding process, providing customizable previews and easy-to-use code snippets that allow fonts to be added to websites quickly. The fonts are optimized for performance, ensuring fast load times, and are designed to be compatible across various devices and browsers, making it a popular choice for creating visually appealing and accessible designs on the web
